If no printer is found, make sure that:
- The main power is turned on.  The network cable is correctly connected.
- The TCP/IP settings on both the PC and the printer are correct.
- The printer and the PC are on the same segment of the network.

Manual setup of the printer port will be required if:
- Printing to a NetWare printing queue.
- Printing to a Windows shared printer
- Using a NetBEUI protocol for printing
- Printing to a different TCP/IP segment
